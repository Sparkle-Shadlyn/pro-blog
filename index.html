I viited Kerala when I was 3(2016)

“My Kerala Tour” Reviewed 4 February 2010 My Kerala tour was so exhilarating that I would call Kerala, the “the canvas of multiple colors”! Kerala is a state located in Southern India. Though Kerala offer many opportunities to make your vacation etched in your mind but Kerala backwaters tour is something which you should not wait to experience. The experience that I gained from my Kerala backwaters tour is so good that I want to once again visit this beautiful land. Kerala Backwaters are a chain of lagoons and lakes that lie parallel to the Arabian Sea. When I landed in this God’s own country I wasn’t surprised to find people of Kerala so friendly and jovial. They were happy to welcome the guests coming to see their land. I want to share my understanding of Kerala people and their culture. I have put together some Kerala Travel Tips for you. Here’s what I have learnt: For International Tourists in Kerala: If you are a tourists coming from countries other than India, then do carry your original Passport and Visa. I advise you also carry some copies of these documents as you might require them. You may also be required to fill up a Currency Declaration Form for the amount of foreign currency you bring in. Credit cards in Kerala: Most of the hotels and restaurants in Kerala accept major credit cards such as AmEx, Masters, Diners and Visa. Communicating in Kerala: Most of the people here talk English, so you can easily communicate with the locals and ask for directions. Weather of Kerala: Kerala has a tropical climate that is neither too hot nor too cold. The state experiences different climate in different regions. High ranges of Kerala remain cool throughout the year whereas plains of Kerala are hot and humid. One personal advice: carry your cottons clothes, straw hats, sunglasses and sun block lotions. If you also plan to visit hill stations in Kerala, then do carry a light sweater with you. Kerala food: There are a variety of cuisines available in Kerala like the Mughlai, Chinese and continental food. You also have a choice for vegetarian and non vegetarian dishes here. Most of the dishes are prepared by adding spices like black pepper, cardamom, cloves, ginger, and cinnamon. You will easily find many bars, pubs and coffee joints in most of the cities of Kerala. No need to worry about hygiene as most of the food outlets prepare food in clean and hygienic surroundings. We suggest you to use packaged drinking water or mineral water for drinking. Hotel and Ticket reservation: Kerala is one of the top favored locations for tourists. We advise you to book your hotel and travel tickets well in advance to avoid last minute rush. Ayurvedic treatments in Kerala: Make sure you visit only authorized resorts and holistic treatment centers for ayurvedic treatmen
